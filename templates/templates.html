About WhichAudit

Our Mission

WhichAudit aims to improve healthcare quality by facilitating the sharing of clinical audits and quality improvement projects across the NHS. We believe that by making audits more accessible, we can:

  • Reduce duplication of effort
  • Spread best practices
  • Accelerate quality improvement
  • Enhance patient care nationwide

How It Works

Our platform allows healthcare professionals to:

  • Share their completed audits with colleagues across the country
  • Discover relevant audits in their specialty
  • Adapt existing audits for local use
  • Collaborate on multi-center audits

Our Team

WhichAudit was developed by a team of NHS clinicians and healthcare IT professionals who recognized the need for better audit sharing across trusts.

Our advisory board includes:

  • Consultants from various specialties
  • Clinical audit specialists
  • Quality improvement leads
  • Data protection officers

Data Protection

We take data protection seriously. All submissions are reviewed to ensure:

  • No patient identifiable information is included
  • All data is appropriately anonymized
  • Projects comply with GDPR requirements

For more details, see our Data Protection Policy.
